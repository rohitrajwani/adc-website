About the Club

Dance is the expression of your soul. We the people of Aavartan Dance Club express anger ,love ,joy ,sadness and even humor. ADC is one of the most glamorous and energetic club which rocks the floor and sets the stage on fire.We have diverse mix of students who are passionate about dance. We do organise various dance events in the campus and even participate in various dance competitions outside the campus.

We are a family, that is bound together by the thread of 'Love For Dance'. It's a place where you come, you dance, and then you never want to leave again.
Dance is about its fun, its frenzy, its excitement and Aavartan Dance Club is about all of the above things

Club Events

Independence Day
15th August 2016

Independence day: This is the day when each and every cell of an Aavartan express their gratitude towards the freedom fighters who sacrificed their life for the independence of nation by dancing with enormous energy.

Revolutionary Night
2nd October 2016

Shaheed Bhagat Singh is an icon who continues to inspire the young, and the young at heart even in the post-modern world of the Internet and funky gadgets. The memories of the legendary revolutionary of India are lingered in our hearts by the fabulous performance given by Aavartan family.
